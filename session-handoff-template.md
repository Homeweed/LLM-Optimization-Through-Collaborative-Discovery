# Session Handoff Template
## Fill in at the end of any meaningful session

> **Why this exists:** The LLM resets every session. You don't. This template is the bridge.
> Without it, compound learning stays theoretical. With it, each session starts from a higher baseline.
>
> **When to use:** Any session where something was learned, built, or decided.
> **Takes:** ~5 minutes. Worth every one.

## How to Use This With the LLM

At session end, paste this template and say:
> "Help me fill in the session handoff. We just finished [brief description]. What should we capture?"

The LLM will help surface what's worth encoding. You make the final call on what goes in.

To bring context forward into the next session:
> "Here's the handoff from my last session: [paste filled template]. We're continuing from here."
